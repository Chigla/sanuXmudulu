Poems written for Mudulu by Sanu

Timro

Ghama lagos yaa nai, mero ujhyali ta timro muskana le ho,

Hawa chalos yaa nai, mero sheetalta ta timro kaala kesha le ho.

Joon lagos yaa nai, mero chamkilo aakash ta timro aankha nai ho,

Sangeet baki rahos yaa nai, mero man mattine ta timro aawaj le nai ho.

Mero mann thik rahos yaa nai, saparne ta timro maya le nai ho,

Aat baaki rahos yaa nai, aasha baki hune ta timro hunu le nai ho.

Her

Pretty is her hair,

As stunning as her eyes,

It's the reason for which,

In my soul she resides.

I have lowkey got butterflies,

Everytime she has smiled,

Compared to her,

The moon's beauty is mild.

I swear I burst with joy,

Every time we have a talk,

Even a lightyear wouldn't be enough,

If she asked me for a walk.

Ek tarfe

Timi naboli basyeu, tara nisabda ma thiye,

Timlai k tha, timl malai nachahada panima timra tii aankha ma faseko thiye.

Ek dui din matra haina, sayau din kurera baseko thiye,

Maya launa najanera po hota, mann ma kura haru ta tei bela dekhi khelairako thiye.

Padhna basda pani kalam le timrai chitra korne gareko thiye,

Timlai po thaha xaina ta, hamro maya ko katha pani maile tyehi kalam le lekhna thalisakeko thiye.

Bas timi magideu

Ma joon matra ta k, taarai lyaidiula,

Bas timi magideu

Ma baani matra ta k, afulai nai feridiula,

Bas timi magideu

Sathi haru ta k, aafna haru sitai daajidiula,

Bas timi magideu

Ful matra ta k, bagaicha nai lyaidiula,

Bas timi magideu

Ek duita matra ta k, hajarau lyaidiula,

Bas timi magideu

Yo mutu matra ta k, saara jeewan sumpidiula,

Bas timi magideu
